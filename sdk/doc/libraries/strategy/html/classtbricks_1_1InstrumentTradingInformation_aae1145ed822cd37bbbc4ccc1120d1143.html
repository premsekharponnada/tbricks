◆ GetPriceCorrectionFactor()

double GetPriceCorrectionFactor ( ) const

Sometimes prices are not properly expressed on a venue, but rather in a convention that is not normalized and that does not fit Tbricks at all. This factor describes how prices are converted by division on the inbound and multiplication on the outbound from Tbricks. The data is normally used by the trading and market data services to properly convert in and outgoing prices.

Returns
Price correction factor or 1.0 if not set
